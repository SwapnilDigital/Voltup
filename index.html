About VoltUp


Taking Charge of Today with One Eye on Tomorrow.

The VoltUp platform is designed to bring you the future of urban mobility now. Imagine a world where you never have to wait for your electric vehicle to recharge. That world is now a reality.
VoltUp is a meticulously built eco-system, one-stop battery swapping platform that promises a future where all 2 and 3 wheeler vehicles can run with zero downtime. VoltUp is transparent, accessible, safe, and most of all instant. A perfect solution for the smart cities of our future.


Vision

Accelerating adoption of sustainable clean energy in daily transport.


Mission

To enable 1 million electric vehicles running on a smart sustainably fuelled battery swapping network.

Technology


VoltUp believes that the future of e-vehicles is driven by data and technology, hence it is continuously evolving with smarter and efficient technology embedded products which not only collects data, but also manages products and provides real-time updates.




Data-Driven :

VoltUp has one of the most technologically advanced charging infrastructures with the ability to monitor battery’s health and availability with real-time data to ensure optimum usage and maximum lifespan.

BMS :

Built to monitor, protect, balance, and communicate; VoltUp batteries are enabled with Battery Management System (BMS) and Telematics to share live information like temperature, voltage, current, distribution, short circuit, etc. over cloud.

Telematics :

VoltUp batteries are integrated with Telematics which helps in tracking crucial data like kilometers traveled, driving quality, live location, speed alert, accident alerts, and geofencing for protection against misuse and theft to ensure safety, optimum battery performance and deliver high value.

Smart VoltUp Lithium-Ion batteries

Why compromise with regular Li-ion or Lead acid batteries when you can get a smarter, powerful and efficient option with VoltUp batteries.







Battery energy density :

VoltUp batteries are not only cheaper but also, are made in the highest quality energy density making it more efficient for longer duration than common Li-ion batteries and Lead acid batteries. The smart VoltUp batteries are an efficient and powerful choice for all e-vehicles.

Zero Maintenance :

VoltUp Li-Ion batteries are low maintenance as compared to Lead-acid batteries that require continuous water checks, terminal cleanings, gravity checks and many more. VoltUp provides its customers with hassle free experience and zero maintenance smart VoltUp Li-Ion batteries which are light in weight and light on pockets. Just swap and leave the rest to the company.

Battery renewal :

VoltUp provides 3 years of unlimited battery swapping with zero downtime on registration. VoltUp smart Li-ion batteries don't need to be replaced every 8 months like Lead acid batteries or every 2 years like common Li-ion batteries thus saving time and cost.

Lightweight :

VoltUp provides smart light weight Li-ion batteries that provide real-time updates and weighs only 12 kgs as compared to ordinary 100kgs batteries…Travel light and travel smart with VoltUp.
